Short Description:
Write down all your expenditures and incomes in your mobile with this handy app.

Full Description:
Not knowing where your hard earned money is spent? Want to keep track of your expenditures? Then this app is made for you. Just enter the amount you've once after installing the app and then start adding the transactions you make. The app will keep track of your expenditures for you. It has ability to directly read the msgs (credit and debit) from your banks and update automatically. Note that this app doesn't require any internet connection and your details will never be shared with anyone. You can even see statistics about your expenditures and export data to SD card after the end of month to a doc file. So what are you waiting for, download the app for free and you'll never miss your money again.
